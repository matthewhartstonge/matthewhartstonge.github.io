Welcome!#

This is the first in a series where I use the Rust programming language to attack a smol problem in order to:

  1. Make the world a little bit safer (TLS everywhere!!)
  2. Help you understand the ins-and-outs of Rust in a practical manner.
  3. Have something practical that you have to play with at the end of the journey.

Today we are going to be looking at Rust concepts and a quick language overview/comparison.
